About Us

Career Guider is a technology firm founded in 2021 and one of very few companies in the MENA region specialized in developing Innovative Online Recruitment Solutions for top enterprises and organizations. We are helping employers and job seekers from UAE, Qatar and other gulf countries find their right match through our intelligent real-time recommendations and around the clock support.
